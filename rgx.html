About RGX 11Z PRO

This amazing bundle was released around a year ago, on October 5, 2021. It's a bit more expensive than the other bundles, but it's worth the price. It's one of the most unique collections in the entirety of Valorant.

Unlike the other collections, it has 5 skin upgrades. This is because the skin has a kill tracker that tracks the number of kills you have every game. The knife in this bundle is also one of the most unique in the game. It's a katana with it's very own unique animations. This is one of my dream knives to have, and I rate this bundle a 8/10.
