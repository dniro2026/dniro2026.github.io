About Forsaken

This great bundle was released on April 23, 2021. It's a collection of skins for the Vandal, Spectre, Classic, Operator. It includes the Forsaken Ritual Blade, which is a great dagger. In total, this bundle costs around 7,100 VP.

This bundle is a classic one. If you don't have a Vandal skin, this is one of the go-to skins. It's very reliable in most games. It's able to give you the spark that you need to start popping off for your team. I personally like the Forsaken Operator because in my experience, I was able to consistently frag out with this skin equipped. I rate this bundle a 7/10.
