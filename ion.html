About Ion 2.0

This bundle was released just recently, on October 18, 2022. It was created as a continuation of the first Ion bundle, which was released around 2 years ago.

The skins it has are very satisfying to use. Their sleek and futuristic look make the user feel as if they're wielding a blaster from Star Wars. My personal favorite from this collection is the Ion 2.0 Vandal. The feeling it gives after hitting a headshot is just amazing. I give this bundle a 7/10.
