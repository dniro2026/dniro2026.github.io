About Glitchpop

This amazing bundle was released more than 2 years ago, on February 2, 2020. It is one of the oldest collection of skins in the game that many people, including me, still use up to this day.

Just like the Forsaken bundle, this bundle is also a classic. It gives a futuristic night-city feel that no other skin collection can replicate. Personally, I think that the Glitchpop Phantom is the best in the game. This was the first skin that I bought, so it holds some sentimental value for me. I rate this bundle an 8/10.
